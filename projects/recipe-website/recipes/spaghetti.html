Odin's Spaghetti

Description:

Spaghetti is one of the most simple but filling dinners you can make. Not only is it delicious, but this recipe easily doubles or even triples. It makes for great leftovers, meal prep, family dinners, or a lazy meal.

Ingredients Needed:

  • 1 lb dry spaghetti
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 tbs olive oil
  • 3/4 cup onion (finely chopped)
  • 4 garlic cloves (chopped)
  • 14 oz petite diced tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 14 oz tomato sauce
  • 1 cup water
  • 2 tbsp italian seasoning
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp cracked black pepper

Recipe Steps:

  1. In a pot on medium heat, add olive oil. Add ground beef, and saute until lightly golden brown. Add onions, salt, pepper, italian seasoning, and galic. Saute until onion is translucent.
  2. Reduce heat to low. Add diced tomatoes, tomato sauce, tomato paste and water. Simmer on low for 10 minutes.
  3. While spaghetti sauce is simmering, cook pasta according to package instructions. When fully cooked, remove from heat and strain.
  4. Combine pasta and spaghetti sauce, toss until fully combined.
  5. Allow to sit for 5-10 minutes before serving.
